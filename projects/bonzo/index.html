Product Challenge

Making Education Engaging at Scale

Educational platforms often struggle with learner drop-off. Bonzo was designed to solve that — a gamified learning system that turns participation into a rewarding, motivating experience through points, badges, streaks, leaderboards, and reward loops.

Built within the Knowledge Platform ecosystem, Bonzo layers game mechanics over curriculum-aligned content — making daily learning feel less like a task and more like a game worth coming back to.

System Design

Gamification System

Six interconnected engagement loops designed to motivate participation, reward consistency, and build long-term learning habits.

Points & Progression
Learners earn XP for every lesson completed, game played, or challenge finished — fuelling visible progress and platform-wide rankings.
Badges & Achievements
A rich library of collectible badges — behavioural, milestone, streak, and consistency — rewarding different learning patterns and accomplishments.
Streaks & Reward Loops
Daily streak tracking with escalating rewards — designed to build consistent learning habits and bring learners back day after day.
Leaderboards
Class, school, and platform-wide leaderboards that surface healthy competition — giving top learners visibility and motivating others to climb.
Quizzes & Mini-Games
Curriculum-aligned interactive games and quizzes that make content review feel playful — not repetitive. Designed for high replay value.
Motivational Feedback
Context-aware feedback moments — celebrations, encouragements, and progress nudges — that keep learners emotionally engaged throughout their journey.
