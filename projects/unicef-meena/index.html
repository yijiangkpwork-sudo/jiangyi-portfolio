Challenge & Role

Designing for Scale and Sensitivity

The project required creating an animation series addressing mental health for children — a sensitive topic demanding both clear visual communication and culturally adaptive delivery. The core challenge was producing a high-quality, emotionally resonant series while building a system capable of multilingual localization without full reproduction.

My role covered creative direction of the animation visual system, design of the AI-assisted production workflow, localization framework design, and oversight of the end-to-end production pipeline.

Localization

Multilingual Delivery

The animation system was designed from the start for multilingual delivery — ensuring that audio, subtitles, and on-screen text elements could be swapped or adapted without triggering full reproduction cycles.

Language and region variants were managed through a modular asset system, enabling localization teams to work in parallel with production.
