Product Overview

A Scalable Singapore-Curriculum Learning System

Coco's Kampong is a complete education product system built as a Learn Smart Singapore and Knowledge Platform joint venture — spanning digital lessons, animated video, picture books, workbooks, interactive games, and reader prototypes aligned to Singapore's primary English and Math curriculum, Grades 1–5.

The world, characters, visual language, and production standards were designed as a reusable, extensible ecosystem — built once, scalable across formats and subjects.
