Product Challenge

From Curriculum to Scalable Product System

Learn Smart Singapore needed a child-friendly learning product that could make Singapore-curriculum content more engaging, visual, and scalable across subjects and markets.

The challenge was to create more than lesson assets — Coco's Kampong needed a repeatable world, character system, and production model that teams could extend across lessons, videos, books, games, marketing, and future interactive products.

My Role & Ownership

Design Lead · Product System · Hands-on Execution

I contributed as Design Lead / Director of Design, shaping the product idea, creative direction, character world, learning experience, production standards, and hands-on execution across digital lessons, videos, readers, workbooks, games, marketing assets, and prototypes.

I worked closely with English and Math subject leads to translate curriculum needs into scalable learner-facing experiences. Once the core system matured, later teams extended the standards into Science and additional markets.
