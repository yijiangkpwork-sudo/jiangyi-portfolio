Overview

A Complete Learning Universe

Coco's Kampong is a Singapore-curriculum education brand designed as a cohesive learning universe — an integrated system of animated videos, picture books, workbooks, games, apps, and interactive reader prototypes, all sharing a unified visual language and character world.

As Design Lead, I owned the product concept support, creative direction, visual system, learning experience design, production standards, and hands-on execution across every format. Academic content writing was owned by subject and content experts.

  • Product concept support and learning experience architecture
  • Creative direction and visual system ownership
  • Production standards and quality pipeline
  • Hands-on design execution across all formats
  • Character and world design
  • Interactive prototype direction
