Overview

Making Finance Accessible for Kids

Pompak is a financial literacy education program designed to give young learners in Pakistan a strong foundation in money, saving, and decision-making — delivered through character-led storytelling, illustrated workbooks, and interactive learning activities.

The design approach prioritised cultural relevance, clear visual communication, and accessible formats to ensure the content resonated with its target audience and was practical for delivery in local school systems.
