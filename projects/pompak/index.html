Product Challenge

Financial Literacy Made Playable

Financial education can feel abstract, especially for younger learners. PomPak transformed money-management concepts into a story-based game where users learn by making decisions, completing challenges, earning rewards, and building a business world.

The game covered saving, budgeting, borrowing, banking, Islamic banking, and business decision-making — spanning three audience groups from children to young adults, in both English and Urdu.

Game Design

Game-Based Learning System

PomPak used a game loop to make financial education more engaging: learners follow characters, complete modules, answer challenges, earn gems, unlock buildings, progress through ranks, and receive a certificate after completion.

Story-Based Learning
Follow characters through real financial decisions and business scenarios.
53 Modules

Interactive Challenges

Gems & Rewards

Unlockable Buildings
Build and upgrade a game world that grows with the learner's progress.
Ranks & Progression
Visible rank advancement keeps learners motivated across modules.

English / Urdu

Character & World Design

Character World & Narrative Design

The game follows entrepreneurial characters growing a business from early financial decisions to larger goals. The visual world helped make saving, budgeting, borrowing, and business planning feel concrete and memorable.

Multi-Format Learning Program

Printed Comic Books & Workbooks

PomPak was not only a digital game — it was a complete multi-format financial literacy learning program. Three printed comic books with workbook-style learning activities were designed for different age groups, extending the game experience into offline and classroom settings through story, characters, financial literacy concepts, and guided exercises.

Children · Ages 9–12
The Pomegranate Tree
Financial Literacy Series · Level 2
Printed comic book and workbook-style activities introducing younger learners to basic financial literacy through story, characters, and guided exercises.
Adolescents · Ages 13–17
Pomegranate Juice
Financial Literacy Series · Level 3
Story-based financial literacy comic and workbook content for teens, covering budgeting, banking, decision-making, and early business thinking.
Youth · Ages 18–29
The Pomegranate Enterprise
Financial Literacy Series · Level 4
Advanced financial literacy comic and workbook content focused on business decisions, Islamic banking, entrepreneurship, and money-management support for young adults.
