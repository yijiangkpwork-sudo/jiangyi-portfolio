Overview

Climate Education for the Next Generation

Green Guardians is a learning product concept designed to introduce children to climate literacy through a character-driven narrative world. The project combines original character IP with curriculum-informed learning design, delivered across video, interactive, and print formats.

The prototype demonstrates how a cohesive brand and character universe can make complex environmental concepts approachable and engaging for young learners.
